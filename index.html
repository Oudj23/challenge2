Pizza


An easy and quick dish ,perfect for any meal

Preparation Time

  • Preaparing Time:30min
  • Cooking Time:1h
  • Total Time:1:30min

Ingredients

  1. Dough
  2. Tomato Sauce
  3. Cheese
  4. Toppings
  5. Olive oil
  6. Herbs

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven
  2. Prepare the Dough
  3. Add Sauce
  4. Add Cheese
  5. Add Toppings
  6. Drizzle with Olive Oil
  7. Add Garlic and Herbs
  8. Finish and Serve
